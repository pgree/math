Detailed Description

template<typename T>
struct stan::math::apply_vector_unary< T, require_std_vector_vt< is_container, T > >

Specialisation for use with nested containers (std::vectors).

For each of the member functions, an std::vector with the appropriate type (vector or scalar) is returned.
